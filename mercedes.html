    • The GT 63 is a high performance, better equipped variant of the GT 4-Door and features the AMG M178 4.0L twin-turbocharged V8 engine from the Mercedes-AMG GT R, which produces 430 kW.

    It has a claimed 100 km/h acceleration time of 3.4 seconds and a top speed of 311 km/h as tested by the manufacturer. The GT 63 also has an optional selectable "Drift Mode" which directs power solely to the rear wheels.

    The car comes standard with an electronically controlled limited-slip rear differential and rear-wheel steering, and features an MCT gearbox with a wet clutch to handle the extra power. The GT 63 uses AMG's multi-chamber air suspension for a sportier, more adaptive ride and has cylinder deactivation mode for increased efficiency.
